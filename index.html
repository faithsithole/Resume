Faith Simunyu

About Me

My name is Faith Simunyu and I am a Medical Illustrator/ Visual Communicator from North Carolina. I have a passion for understanding the chemistry and biology of disease in addition to the artistic expression of science. My goal is to create visuals (in the form of illustrations and animations) to render science beautifully, as researchers, physicians and educators understand it.
